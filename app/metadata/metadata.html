Metadata

Information about our raw and summarized data types, as well as field-level metadata, can be found from the links below.

File Description
Summarized Supports estimates of phenophase onset, duration and end.
Raw Each row in this data type represents the status of one phenophase for one individual plant or animal species at a given site, on a single date and time.
Site Level Provides information on the mean first and the mean last positive observations for a given species and phenophase within a calendar year that are captured at a site within a user-defined interval.
